The Partnership

Why Eli Schwartz + Foundation.

Two firms, one playbook for the brands that need both: SEO that compounds and content that distributes.

B2B brands that depend on organic search face a sharper problem every quarter. Algorithms shift, AI search reshuffles results, and the gap between "good content" and content that compounds keeps widening. The brands that win build search into their product and editorial, not as an afterthought.

That's the gap this partnership closes. Eli Schwartz brings the Product-Led SEO methodology he wrote the book on. He's used the same approach at SurveyMonkey, Shutterstock, and Mixpanel. Foundation brings the editorial team, distribution motion, and 12-month content plan. You get both: the strategy and the engine, in one engagement.

Most SEO consultants stop at the strategy slide. Most agencies start when the strategy is already locked. We meet in the middle. Eli sets the direction. Foundation ships the content that earns rankings. Your team walks away with an organic engine that keeps compounding after we're gone.
