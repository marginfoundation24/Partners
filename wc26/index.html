How the draw worked

The 48 qualified countries were split by FIFA ranking (1 Apr 2026 official update): Tier 1 = ranks 1–13 · Tier 2 = ranks 14–27 · Tier 3 = ranks 30–88. Each of the 12 teams was randomly dealt 1 + 1 + 2. The 65 players were randomly split into the 12 teams. The draw is final — luck of the draw is part of the game.

How scoring updates

In live mode this page pulls official match data (scores, goal minutes, scorers, cards, penalties, own goals) from API-Football every time it's opened, and re-checks every 5 minutes while open. Finished matches are cached in your browser so repeat visits are fast and light on the request quota. Every point is itemised — expand any team on the leaderboard to audit the maths down to the minute of each yellow card.
